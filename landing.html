The Solution

One platform. Three agents.
Zero barriers to access.

CardioGuard AI combines a trained cardiac risk classifier, Claude AI, and WhatsApp into a single platform that works on any smartphone, in Hindi, with no app install required.

🧠

Antigravity-22 Risk Engine

A GradientBoosting classifier trained on 22 clinical and lifestyle features — age, cholesterol, blood pressure, diabetes, smoking, family history and more. Returns a risk score (0–100%) with SHAP explainability for every patient.

AUC 0.81 • SHAP Explained
🤖

Hridai Brain — Claude AI

Powered by Anthropic’s Claude Sonnet, the Hridai Brain generates personalised wellness guidance in Hindi and English for every risk assessment — explaining why the score is high and what the patient can do today.

Bilingual • Empathetic • Claude Sonnet
📱

ASHA Dispatch Agent

When a patient scores ≥70% (Critical Deviation), the system automatically dispatches a WhatsApp alert to the assigned ASHA coordinator. No manual escalation. No delays. Priority queue for health camps via the batch API.

WhatsApp • Auto-Escalation • Zero Delay
How It Works

From risk data to ASHA alert
in under 3 seconds.

1

Patient Fills Form

22 health parameters entered via a mobile-friendly web form on cardioguardai.in — no app, no registration, no device required.

2

AI Scores the Risk

The Antigravity-22 model computes a cardiac risk score instantly. SHAP identifies the top 5 risk drivers specific to that patient.

3

Claude Explains in Hindi

The Hridai Brain generates personalised, empathetic guidance in Hindi and English — what the score means and what action to take.

4

ASHA Gets Alerted

For critical cases (≥70%), a WhatsApp alert fires automatically to the ASHA worker. Home visit scheduled. Referral initiated.
